Hey there! My name is Colin Green. I am 26 years old. I was born and raised in Central Florida, and I currently reside in Atlanta, Georgia. Ever since I was little, I have loved video games, computers, and technology.

I recently started a Coding Bootcamp at Georgia Tech, and I am very excited to complete it! I'm about halfway through it, at the moment. I have completed many assignments in which I had to create a functioning website with a certain goal in mind, such as Hangman, Trivia, a GIF finder, and many more. I have even completed my first group project! Feel free to check out my Portfolio!

I've been recognized as one of the top 40 players in the world for 6 years in a row now. I also enjoy spending time with my girlfriend, watching anime, playing World of Warcraft, and speedrunning other video games.
